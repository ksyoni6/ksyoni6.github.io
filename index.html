Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my daily weight and calories?

Open Calkit and tap the "Add food Record" button to add your daily entries. Simply enter your weight and calorie intake for the day.

How does the 7-day average work?

The app automatically calculates your average calorie intake over the last 7 days, giving you a smoother view of your eating patterns.

Can I delete past entries?

Yes, tap on any previous entry to delete your weight or calorie data.
