Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Calkit ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Health Data: Weight measurements and calorie intake that you manually enter
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App (features used, frequency of use)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Performance Data: Crash reports, performance metrics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • App Functionality: To display your weight and calorie data, calculate averages, and show weekly trends
  • Data Storage: All personal health data is stored locally on your device
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage analytics to improve App performance and features

Important: Your Data Stays Private

Your weight and calorie data never leaves your device. We do not have access to your personal health information.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All personal health data (weight, calories) is stored securely on your device
  • Data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption methods
  • We do not have access to your personal health data

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Share Personal Data

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • We do not share your health data with third parties
  • Anonymous analytics may be shared with analytics providers

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: View all your data within the App
  • Deletion: Delete individual entries or all data through App settings
  • Analytics Opt-Out: Opt out through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics

Children's Privacy

Calkit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all local data
  • We do not retain copies of your personal health data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@calkit.app

Compliance: This Privacy Policy complies with Apple App Store Review Guidelines, CCPA, GDPR (where applicable), and COPPA.
